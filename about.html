ABOUT

Mitchell's opened its doors in the winter of 1989 in Cape Coral, Florida. Founded by Rosemary and Walter Jenkins, two highschool sweethearts bonded by their love of upscale food. Named after their son, The quaint restaurant moved to Nashville after the devastating hurricane Charley in 2004. Made famous for their hand breaded lightly fried hen strippies and zesty tomato and vinegar relish.
